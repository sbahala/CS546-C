Trip to Niagara Falls

May 20, 2023, 11:00 AM

Seeing Niagara Falls in its entirety in beauty and an extensively humbling experience. A misty shower touched my face as I stood at the edge of Horseshoe Falls on the US side, serving as subtle reminder of the power of nature that was in front of me.

An adventure in and of itself was the Maid of the Mist boat cruise. It was quite breathtaking to watch the water pour down in such a powerful manner. I tool some amazing pictures on the observation deck, with the rainbow formations in the mist enhancing the amazing view.

The evening brought a different perspective as the falls lit up in a kaleidoscope of colors. This luminous display against the night sky was a fitting end to a day filled with winder. Niagara Falls is not just a destination, it's a natural phenomenon that reminds us of the natural beauty that our planet holds.

Niagara Falls, USA

An adventure among the Falls

May 22, 2023, 09:00 AM

I embarked on a journey through Ricketts Glen State Park, a breathtaking region where nature has painted a panorama of flowing waterfalls and peaceful streams.

With each step, I was in amazement of the park's 21 magnificent waterfalls, each with its own charm and beauty.

The experience at Ganoga Falls was the peak of this waterfall journey. Long before I saw its hazy plume, the sound of the 94-foot-tall falls boomed in my ears. It was a truly thrilling moment that rewarded the hiker for all of his hard work.

Ricketts Glen State Park

Reflection by Lake Jean

May 22, 2023, 05:00 PM

Lake Jean was bathed in a warm glow as the sun started to set. I watched the light dance over the lake and listened to the soft sound of the waves as I spent the evening in serene reflection. Moments were spent with local fishermen and more than just a walk, the day at Ricketts Glen was a time of communion with the natural world.

Knoebels Amusement Resort

May 24, 2023, 10:00 AM

A visit to Knoebels Amusement Resort brings a delightful break from nature's serenity with its thrilling rides and friendly atmosphere. As America's largest free amusement park, it offers a charming mix of classic rides and modern attractions.

With a full day spent riding, playing games and indulging in classic amusement park, Knoebels didn't just entertain me but also created beautiful memories which will be cherished for years to come.

Knoebels Amusement Resort, PA

Bloomsburg Fair

September 26, 2023, 12:00 AM

The Bloomsburg Fair, a vibrant mixture of culture and tradition, is an annual event not to be missed. It is one of the Pennsylvania's largest county fair.

It was a showcase of agricultural heritage, live entertainment, art and local cuisine. To me it was a delightful journey through the heart of Pennsylvania's countryside charm.

Bloomsburg, PA
